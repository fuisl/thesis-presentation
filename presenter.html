▶ Presenter Console

FGS — Thesis Defense. This console shows your current slide, the next slide, your speaker notes, and a timer. Opening the audience window pops out a clean full-screen deck that stays in sync as you navigate.

  1. Extend your display (projector as a second screen, not mirrored).
  2. Click below — a new window opens. Drag it to the projector.
  3. Make that window full-screen (F11, or ⌃⌘F on macOS).
  4. Come back here and drive with → / ← or the on-screen buttons.
